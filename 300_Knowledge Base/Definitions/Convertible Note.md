# Convertible Note

```ad-summary
title: Definition
collapse: open
A debt instrument that converts into equity at a later financing round, often with a discount or cap.
```

```ad-tldr
title: Context
collapse: open
- Important for flexible early-stage financing.  
- Appears in [[seed]] and [[bridge]] rounds.
```
