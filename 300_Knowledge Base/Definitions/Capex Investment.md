# Capex Investment

```ad-summary
title: Definition
collapse: open
Capital expenditures made by companies to acquire, upgrade, or maintain physical assets.
```

```ad-tldr
title: Context
collapse: open
- Important in financial analysis to distinguish from operating expenses.  
- Appears in business growth and infrastructure scaling.
```
