# General Partners (in a VC)

```ad-summary
title: Definition
collapse: open
The managers of a venture capital fund who make investment decisions and manage the fund’s portfolio.
```

```ad-tldr
title: Context
collapse: open
- Important because they control fund strategy and carry.  
- Appear as fund managers in limited partnership structures.
```
