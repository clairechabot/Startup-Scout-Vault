# Accelerators

```ad-summary
title: Definition
collapse: open
Programs that provide early-stage startups with mentorship, resources, and sometimes funding in exchange for equity, usually in a fixed-term cohort.
```

```ad-tldr
title: Context
collapse: open
- Important for helping startups validate business models and prepare for fundraising.  
- Appear in early-stage startup ecosystems and seed funding rounds.
```
