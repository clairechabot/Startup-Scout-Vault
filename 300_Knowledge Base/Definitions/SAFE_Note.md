# SAFE Note

```ad-summary
title: Definition
collapse: open
A Simple Agreement for Future Equity (SAFE) is a contract that allows investors to invest money in a startup now in exchange for future equity at a later priced round.
```

```ad-tldr
title: Context
collapse: open
- Important because it simplifies seed financing without debt or valuation negotiations.  
- Appears in pre-seed and seed rounds.
```
