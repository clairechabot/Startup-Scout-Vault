# Burn Rate

```ad-summary
title: Definition
collapse: open
The rate at which a startup spends cash before generating positive cash flow.
```

```ad-tldr
title: Context
collapse: open
- Important for calculating runway and fundraising needs.  
- Appears in investor reports and financial models.
```
