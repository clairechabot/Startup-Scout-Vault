# Topic
Introduction to paradigm shifts in physics, astronomy, and biology.

```ad-summary
Early scientists challenged existing dogma by redefining what counted as evidence. Einstein discussed how paradigms shape not only research questions but acceptable methods.

```

# Notes

- Paradigms organize the “rules” of a scientific worldview.
    
- Students discussed how Copernicus questioned the Ptolemaic model.
    
- Einstein emphasized theory-driven vs. empirically-driven revolutions.
    
- Curie’s radiological work was presented as a modern example of overturning assumptions about matter.
